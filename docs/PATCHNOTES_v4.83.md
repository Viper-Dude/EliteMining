**Release Date:** 2025-01-29

## New Features & Improvements

### VoiceAttack Controls
- Added automated multi-cycle laser mining: Split laser firing time into multiple cycles (1-10 repeats) to prevent weapon overheating and maintain optimal mining efficiency throughout the entire mining sequence

### Voiceattack Profile
- Updated to v4.8.3 - Supports automated multi-cycle laser mining with configurable repeat count (included in the installer and zip file)

## Important Notes

### Ship Presets
After updating, existing ship presets may need to be re-saved if timers or toggles don't load correctly. Simply load your preset, verify all settings are correct, then right-click and save again to update the preset file.

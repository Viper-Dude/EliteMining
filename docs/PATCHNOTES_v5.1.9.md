**Release Date:** July 02, 2026

## New Features & Improvements
* **Hotspots Finder** — Added PowerPlay search filter and PowerPlay info column to the search results
* **Star Systems** — Added PowerPlay search filter and PowerPlay info column to the search results; right-click or double-click a system to fetch its PowerPlay data from Inara
* **Help Links** — Added a "?" next to each tab that opens the matching section of the online guide

#### Important Notes

* The PowerPlay column fills in instantly for systems you jump into yourself, and automatically from EDDN (live data from other commanders) for the rest — only while the app is running, so that part of the coverage can take hours to days to build up. If a system shows **No data ↗**, double-click it to open Inara and fetch the info automatically.

## Voiceattack 
* No update - Current version is 5.1.7

#### Important Notes

* If and after updating the VoiceAttack profile, always verify your keyboard, joystick, and HOTAS bindings to ensure they are configured correctly.

* For the Prospector sequence to work, make sure your joystick/HOTAS fire button isn’t bound to the Prospector Limpet Controller fire control (Game Controller Options > Weapons). Shared bindings will interrupt the sequence.

**Release Date:** July 20, 2026

## New Features & Improvements

- Added **"Edit Ring Type..."** to the **Hotspots Finder** right-click menu, allowing you to add or correct a ring's type if missing or incorrect.

- Expanded the built-in hotspot database from 60,000+ to **80,000+** mining locations. This updates automatically the first time you launch after installing — your own scans, visits, and edits are kept, and a backup of your database is made first just in case.

- First launch after this update will do a one-time scan of your journal files to rebuild your hotspot and visit data — this may take a moment.

- **Using the ZIP (portable) version?** This hotspot database update only merges safely through the installer. If you extract this ZIP over an existing install, it will replace your saved database, including your visited systems and any manual edits. **Import Journal History** in Settings can rebuild your visited systems and your own scan results from your journal files afterward, but manual edits and notes aren't stored in journals and can't be recovered that way. For a safe update that keeps everything, use the installer instead.

### Bug Fixes

- Fixed the **Mining Commodity** tab search not sorting by distance correctly during galaxy-wide searches.
- Fixed the **Star Systems** Power State filter displaying options that never returned results, and added the missing **"Unoccupied"** option.
- Fixed **Star Systems** occasionally losing part of their saved Powerplay data when refreshed from Inara.
- Fixed the **Commodity Market** **Fleet Carrier** filter incorrectly including Stronghold Carriers.
